China Visa Requirements 中国签证要求

Essential information for obtaining your Chinese visa

Basic Requirements

  • Valid passport with at least 6 months remaining validity and at least 2 blank visa pages
  • Completed COVA visa application form (online form at cova.mfa.gov.cn)
  • Recent passport-sized photo (33mm x 48mm)
  • Photocopy of the bio-page of the passport
  • Photocopy of the latest Chinese visa or Chinese residence permit (if applicable)
  • Proof of residence (e.g., driver's license, utility bill, bank bill)
  • Where You Stay Form (handwritten signature required)

Visa Types

  • Tourist Visa (L) - For tourism purposes
  • Business Visa (M) - For commercial and trade activities
  • Student Visa (X) - For studying in China
  • Work Visa (Z) - For employment in China
  • Family Visit Visa (Q1/Q2) - For visiting family members
  • Transit Visa (G) - For transiting through China

Processing Time

  • Regular service: 4 business days
  • Express service: 3 business days (for urgent situations)
  • Note: Processing times may vary based on individual circumstances

Official Information Source

For the most up-to-date and accurate visa information, please visit the official Chinese Embassy website.

Visit Official Embassy Website

Additional Information

  • All visa applications must be submitted in person or through an authorized agent
  • Payment methods accepted: Credit card (Master or Visa), money order, or cashier's check
  • Face mask is required when visiting the visa office
  • Office hours: 9:30-14:30 (Monday to Friday, closed on weekends and holidays)
