Regional Cuisines

Sichuan Cuisine

Known for its bold flavors and liberal use of garlic and spicy peppers, particularly the unique numbing-spiciness of Sichuan peppercorns.

  • Mapo Tofu
  • Kung Pao Chicken
  • Twice-Cooked Pork
  • Hot Pot

Cantonese Cuisine

Emphasizes fresh ingredients and subtle flavors, with dim sum being a popular dining experience that showcases the variety of small dishes.

  • Dim Sum
  • Sweet and Sour Pork
  • Char Siu
  • Steamed Fish

Northern Cuisine

Features wheat-based staples and hearty dishes, reflecting the cooler climate and agricultural traditions of northern China.

  • Peking Duck
  • Hand-Pulled Noodles
  • Dumplings
  • Steamed Buns
