The Art of Light and Shadow

2000+ Years of History

Historical Development

Chinese shadow play is one of the oldest forms of storytelling and entertainment, using light and shadow to bring stories to life through intricately crafted leather puppets.

Regional Styles

Different styles of shadow play across China.

  • Tangshan Shadow Play (唐山皮影)
  • Huanxian Shadow Play (环县皮影)
  • Shandong Shadow Play (山东皮影)
  • Sichuan Shadow Play (四川皮影)
  • Shaanxi Shadow Play (陕西皮影)
"灯影戏中见人生，皮影戏里看世界" (See life in shadow play, view the world through leather puppets)
