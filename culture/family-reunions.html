Understanding Chinese Family Reunions

Family reunions, known as 团圆 (tuán yuán), are central to Chinese culture, symbolizing unity, harmony, and the continuation of family traditions. These gatherings serve as essential occasions for maintaining family bonds and passing down cultural heritage.
