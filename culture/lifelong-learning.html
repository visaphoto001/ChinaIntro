Philosophical Foundations

Lifelong learning has deep roots in Chinese philosophical traditions, emphasizing continuous personal development and intellectual growth throughout one's life.

Confucian Perspective

The Confucian emphasis on continuous self-improvement and moral cultivation.

  • Self-Cultivation (修身)
  • Moral Development
  • Intellectual Growth
  • Social Responsibility
  • Role of the Scholar

Daoist Approach

The Daoist view of learning as a natural process of discovery and harmony.

  • Intuitive Understanding

  • Inner Wisdom

Buddhist Influence

The Buddhist concept of continuous learning and enlightenment.

  • Compassionate Action
  • Spiritual Practice
  • Wisdom Development
