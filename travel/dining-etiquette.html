Chinese Dining Etiquette

Master the art of Chinese dining etiquette for a more authentic cultural experience

Table Seating

  • Guest of honor sits facing the door
  • Host sits opposite the guest of honor
  • Other guests sit according to seniority
  • Wait for seating arrangement guidance

Chopstick Etiquette

  • Hold chopsticks properly
  • Don't stick chopsticks in rice
  • Don't point with chopsticks
  • Use serving chopsticks for shared dishes

Tea Culture

  • Tap table to thank for tea service
  • Keep others' cups filled
  • Accept tea when offered
  • Hold cup with both hands when receiving

Common Dining Phrases

Chinese Pinyin English Usage
请慢用 Qǐng màn yòng Please enjoy your meal Host to guests
谢谢 Xièxiè Thank you After being served
干杯 Gānbēi Cheers During toasts
很好吃 Hěn hǎochī Very delicious Complimenting food

Important Tips

  • Wait for the host to start eating
  • Try a bit of everything offered
  • Leave some food on your plate
  • Accept food/drink when offered
  • Use both hands when receiving items
  • Express appreciation for the meal
