Chinese Greeting Customs

Understanding proper greeting etiquette is essential for respectful interactions in China

Basic Greetings

  • Nod slightly when greeting
  • Use appropriate titles (先生/女士)
  • Maintain moderate eye contact
  • Show respect through body language

Handshakes

  • Accept handshakes when offered
  • Use a gentle grip
  • Shake briefly (2-3 seconds)
  • Follow the senior person's lead

Business Greetings

  • Exchange business cards properly
  • Use formal titles
  • Show respect for hierarchy
  • Maintain professional demeanor

Common Greeting Phrases

Chinese Pinyin English Usage
你好 Nǐ hǎo Hello General greeting
早上好 Zǎoshang hǎo Good morning Morning greeting
晚上好 Wǎnshang hǎo Good evening Evening greeting
您好 Nín hǎo Hello (formal) Respectful greeting

Important Tips

  • Always greet the most senior person first in a group
  • Use formal greetings in business settings
  • Avoid physical contact beyond handshakes
  • Show respect through proper body language
  • Learn basic Chinese greetings to show cultural respect
  • Be patient and observe local customs
